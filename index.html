About

Learn more about our journey

We are a software company, specialising in embedded communications software and related server-based applications. With a mature product range, we have supplied our own SIM and OTA platform for the last five years. Boasting a worldwide customer base, we serve over 45 mobile network operators with offices in Bangor (HQ), Belfast and Cape Town.


We are working on pioneering technology to enable the connected Internet of Things. We believe that every device can be connected, with no compromise on quality, security or privacy. Our challenges are novel, and not yet solved. We need bright problem solvers to bring our ideals to a reality.
